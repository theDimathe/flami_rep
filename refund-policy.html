Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your subscription. If the service doesn't meet your expectations, you can request a refund according to the rules below.

Eligibility

  • Refund requests are accepted within 14 days after the initial purchase.
  • Only first-time subscription purchases are eligible for a full refund.
  • Renewal charges may be reviewed individually depending on usage and local law.

How to request a refund

  1. Contact support and provide your purchase receipt or transaction ID.
  2. Describe the reason for your request.
  3. Wait for confirmation from our billing team (usually within 3 business days).

Important notes

Refund processing time depends on your payment provider and can take 5–10 business days after approval.
