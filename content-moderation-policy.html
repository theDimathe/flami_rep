Content Moderation Policy

We are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment. Content is reviewed by automated systems and, when necessary, by human moderators.

Prohibited content

  • Illegal, exploitative, or non-consensual material.
  • Hate speech, harassment, threats, or promotion of violence.
  • Attempts to generate harmful instructions or abusive interactions.

Enforcement actions

  • Content removal or blocking.
  • Temporary account restrictions.
  • Permanent suspension for severe or repeated violations.

Appeals

If you believe moderation was applied incorrectly, you can contact support to request a manual review.
